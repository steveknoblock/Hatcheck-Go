Roles
A role is two things: a definition (the set of capability grants it carries) and a membership (which principals hold it). Assigning a role mints one capability per grant for the principal; removing it revokes them. Editing a role's grants below retroactively applies to everyone who already holds it.
Role definition
Grants are the capability templates a role carries. Adding one here immediately issues that capability to every principal currently holding the role below; removing one revokes it from all of them.
